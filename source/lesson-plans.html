Intro to Informatics and User-Centered Design

Lesson Overview

This lesson plan introduces the concept of Informatics and its applications, followed by an introduction to User Centered Design, and some small group activities for practicing UCD.

User Centered Design

User Centered Design is a collection of processes in which the needs, wants, and limitations of end users are given extensive attention at teach stage of the design process.
